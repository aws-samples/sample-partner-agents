Business Problem:
AnyCompany Financial is currently running their core trading platform on-premises with aging
infrastructure that is now five years old. Their VMware licenses are coming up for renewal

sub-minute failover. The combined pressure of expiring VMware costs, aging hardware,
compliance requirements, and scalability needs during peak market hours has made cloud
migration a board-level priority for 2026.

Project Scope and Use Case:
The primary use case is Application Migration — specifically a lift-and-shift modernization
of AnyCompany Financial's core trading platform from on-premises VMware to AWS. Scope includes:
45 VMs migrating to ECS/EKS for containerized microservices, 3 Oracle databases moving
to Aurora PostgreSQL, and a real-time event streaming layer migrating to Amazon MSK.
Sub-minute RPO/RTO using Multi-AZ Aurora with cross-region disaster recovery is required.

Solution and Delivery Model:
We're proposing a Managed Services delivery model where our team handles the migration
end-to-end and provides ongoing operations. Solution description: AWS-native managed

migration (Oracle to Aurora PostgreSQL), event streaming (Amazon MSK), and Multi-AZ
disaster recovery architecture. Estimated monthly AWS spend post-migration: $35,000
with a 1-year Compute Savings Plan committed.
