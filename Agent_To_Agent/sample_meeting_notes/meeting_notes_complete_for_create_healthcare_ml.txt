Business Problem:
AnyCompany Health's clinical staff currently spends an average of 4.2 hours per week per
provider on chart preparation and documentation review. A 2026 internal study showed
that 38% of preventable readmissions could be flagged earlier if discharge summaries
and lab trend data were synthesized in real time. Their existing on-premises NLP
pipeline (Spark on bare-metal) takes 6+ hours per batch, missing the same-day
clinical decision window. Recent HIPAA enforcement actions in the region have
also raised the bar on PHI handling — AnyCompany Health's compliance team requires any new
ML workload to support BAA coverage, audit logging, and de-identification at rest.
The combined pressure of clinician burnout, readmission penalties under CMS HRRP,
and tighter PHI controls has made an AI-driven clinical-decision-support platform
a top-three CIO priority for fiscal year 2027.

Project Scope and Use Case:
The primary use case is AI Machine Learning and Analytics. Scope includes:
fine-tuning an Amazon Bedrock foundation model on AnyCompany Health's 8 years of
de-identified discharge notes, deploying real-time inference for readmission risk
scoring on Amazon SageMaker, ingesting HL7/FHIR feeds from their existing electronic
health record system via AWS HealthLake, and surfacing predictions back to clinicians
through a clinician-facing chat assistant embedded in their existing physician portal.
Data residency must stay in us-east-2; cross-region replication only for backup. All
inference traffic will go through PrivateLink — no public endpoints.

Solution and Delivery Model:
We're proposing a SaaS or PaaS delivery model. AnyCompany Health will own and operate
the AWS account; our team delivers the platform as a managed software stack with
quarterly model retraining and ongoing performance monitoring. Solution description:
AWS-native clinical decision support platform combining Amazon Bedrock for
foundation model fine-tuning, Amazon SageMaker for inference, AWS HealthLake for
FHIR ingestion, Amazon OpenSearch Service for clinical knowledge retrieval, and
a clinician-facing assistant. Comprehensive HIPAA controls including AWS KMS
customer-managed keys, VPC endpoint isolation, and CloudTrail data-event logging.
Estimated incremental monthly AWS spend: $22,000 above their current baseline,
ramping to $38,000 by month nine as more clinics onboard.
