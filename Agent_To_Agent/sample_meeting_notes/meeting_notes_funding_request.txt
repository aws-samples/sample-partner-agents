Meeting Notes — Migration Planning Workshop with NovaTech Solutions
Date: May 18, 2026
Attendees: Sarah Chen (VP Engineering, NovaTech), Mike Rodriguez (CTO, NovaTech),
            Ming Zhu (AWS Partner SA), Christina Passmore (AWS Partner Account Manager)

Background:
NovaTech Solutions is a fintech that has been working with us for ~3 months. The opportunity is qualified
and they are ready to commit to a migration. This meeting was a planning workshop to scope the work and
identify funding opportunities. Mike (CTO) confirmed that budget is approved internally for the migration
but they are looking for AWS funding to offset the migration tooling and training costs.

Discussion Highlights:

Migration Scope Confirmed:
- 45 VMs (Java microservices) → ECS/EKS
- 3 Oracle databases → Aurora PostgreSQL
- Real-time event streaming layer → Amazon MSK
- Estimated effort: ~6 months end-to-end, ~$250K in third-party migration tooling

Budget & Spend Profile:
- Steady-state AWS spend post-migration: $35K/month
- They are willing to commit to a 1-year Compute Savings Plan
- One-time migration costs estimated at $400K (tooling + training + parallel run)

Funding Discussion (this is the main reason for the meeting):
- Mike specifically asked: "What AWS funding programs are available to help with our migration?"
- Christina mentioned MAP (Migration Acceleration Program) as the natural fit
- We discussed MAP eligibility criteria — they qualify based on workload size and commitment
- Mike asked about funding amounts. We need to confirm specifics with the AWS rep
- Sarah asked if they could also use POC funding for the Aurora benchmark testing they want to do
- Christina noted that the AWS rep will need to formally submit the funding request

Key Asks from NovaTech:
- They want a written estimate of total AWS funding they could receive (MAP + POC)
- They need help building the migration business case for their board (CFO presenting end of month)
- They want a proposed funding package by next Friday — this is a hard deadline
- Sarah asked for AWS' migration tooling recommendations (DMS, MGN, Application Migration Service)

Decisions Made:
- We will submit a MAP funding request on their behalf this week
- Christina will engage the AWS Solutions Architect team to estimate funding amounts
- We will deliver a board-ready migration business case by end of next week
- Aurora POC can start as soon as funding is in place (likely early June)
